Personality

When someone first interacts with him, Jamil gives off the vibe of someone who tries to avoid attention or the spotlight. Mainly because his upbringing caused him to be aware of how he acts in social settings in order to avoid any potential problems. Even though he wishes more than anything for the world to know the real him. However, he is much more than what he lets on, smarts and skills wise, and there are occasions where he will show off this part of him with no qualms. Having to hide his true potential however has fostered a resentment in him, especially towards Kalim. Unfortunately for this snake, he has to keep an eye on him constantly and it makes him so tired that it makes him borderline apathetic to practically anything else outside of his duties.

Backstory

Jamil was born into the Viper family and they have been servants for the Asim household for generations. Due to this, Jamil's obligation has been to perform the role of attendant, security guard, and companion to Kalim since a very young age. Jamil´s role in this companionship is distinctly protocolary, with rules that Jamil is bound to follow, like not overshadowing Kalim in any way or giving even his life if it is required to keep him safe.

Jamil doesn't want to awaken the wrath of Kalim´s father against his own family, so he was forced to develop his skills to a high degree in order to protect Kalim, making him an expert in many areas. Jamil was also instructed by his parents to never perform better than Kalim and to keep a low profile. To ensure the Asim family stay content, he was required not to draw any attention to himself. This has fostered mixed feelings in Jamil from a young age, as he is disgusted and resentful that his family is subjected to such unfairness and control by the Asim family.

Unique Magic

Snake Whisper - He has the ability to hypnotize someone to do whatever he commands. Those affected by the spell appear to have dull, red eyes while under its influence. It's possible for Jamil to use this spell on multiple people at once. Because it involves mind-control, this signature spell is considered to be one of the more powerful ones, requiring a higher level of magical ability than others. As a result, however, excessive use of this spell can cause rapid blot accumulation.
